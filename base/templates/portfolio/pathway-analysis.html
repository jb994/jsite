  PORTFOLIO

  Ontology & Pathway Analysis



In conjunction with information from other analyses, I can take the important genes or proteins of a dataset and put them through a pathway enrichment to determine the most pathways most imvolved in a given disease state or drug treatment.

Differentially expressed gene lists, WGCNA modules of genes, list of differentially spliced genes, or any other list of genes connected with a given treatment can be put through a pathway enrichment to uncover the underlying mechanisms at play.



Various different databases can be referenced for pathway enrichment in order to get the data most relevant to the treatment. A few examples of databases available to perform enrichment with include:
    - KEGG 2021
    - GO Biological Pathways
    - Reactome
    - Wikipathways
    - PPI Hub Proteins
    - etc.
