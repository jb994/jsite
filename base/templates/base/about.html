About This Guy

I was born and raised in Canada and grew up in Edmonton, Alberta. I got my bachelors degree from the University of Alberta in mathematics in 2019 and afterwards I decided I wanted to obtain a Masters degree in Europe. Having travelled extensively in my life I've learned that I love experienceing new cultures and places and wanted to try living in a new place for the duration of my studies. In 2020 I got into the University of Freiburg, Germany, for a Masters in Computer Science, however, the covid-19 pandemic saw world travel come to a halt and so the first two semesters of my degree I ended up living and studying in Canada but travelling to Germany to write exams. Finally in July 2021 I moved to Germany and lived there until October 2022 when I completed my degree with a specialization in Artificial Intelligence.

I love to travel and have visited over 30 countries so far (the adjacent picture is of me in Zion national park, Utah in 2023). In my personal time I enjoy ice hockey (my favorite team is the Edmonton Oilers if they ever manage to win something in my lifetime but they've yet to win anything in my lifetime...). Additionally, I speak english and german and am currently attending classes to learn spanish.
